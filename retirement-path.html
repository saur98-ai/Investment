The Numbers Don't Lie

Why Every Year of Delay Costs You Crores

01

Start at 30 vs 40

₹10,000/month SIP from age 30 builds ~₹3.5 Cr by 60. Starting at 40 builds just ~₹1 Cr. A 10-year delay costs ₹2.5 Crore.

02

Inflation Is Silent

At 6% inflation, today's ₹1 lakh monthly expense becomes ₹3.2 lakh in 20 years. Your retirement corpus must account for this.

03

Healthcare Costs Spike

Medical inflation in India runs at 12–15% annually. Adequate health cover and a dedicated healthcare fund are non-negotiable.

04

Your Legacy Matters

Without a will and proper nominations, courts and disputes can tie up your life's work for years. A simple estate plan prevents this entirely.
