Step 01 — India's IFSC
Gujarat · SEZ · Global Finance Hub
What is GIFT City?
Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City) is India's only International Financial Services Centre (IFSC). It operates like an offshore jurisdiction within India — allowing USD-denominated transactions, foreign investments, and global financial products with significant tax advantages.
Key benefit: GIFT City funds enjoy 10-year tax holiday on profits

Step 02 — The Tax Edge
Tax Holiday · No STT · No GST
Why GIFT City Saves You Money
GIFT City-based funds and entities enjoy a 10-year income tax holiday, zero STT, zero CTT, no GST on IFSC services, and no stamp duty — making it one of the most tax-efficient investment jurisdictions in Asia.
