What Are Unlisted Securities?
Unlisted securities are equity shares of companies that haven't yet listed on stock exchanges. You buy directly from existing shareholders or the company. Think NSDL, PhonePe, OYO, or your favourite startup — before they hit the markets.
Key insight: You get direct demat-held shares — no pooling, full ownership

Risks You Must Understand
Unlisted shares are illiquid — you can't sell them on an exchange anytime. There's no guaranteed IPO timeline, and valuations can be tricky. That's why careful stock selection and trusted sourcing is everything.
