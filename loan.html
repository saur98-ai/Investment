What is Loan Against Security?
LAS is a facility where you pledge your financial assets — mutual funds, stocks, bonds — as collateral and get a loan against them. Your investments keep earning returns while you get the cash you need.

Step 03 — The Process
Simple · Fast · Flexible
How Does LAS Work?
You pledge your securities online, get a credit limit sanctioned, and draw funds as needed — just like a credit card. Interest is charged only on the amount used. Repay anytime, unpledge your assets, and you're free.
Interest benefit: Pay interest only on the amount drawn, not the full limit
