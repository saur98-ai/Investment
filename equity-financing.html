Step 01 — Understanding Equity Capital
Raise · Dilute · Scale · Exit
What Is Equity Financing?
Equity financing involves raising capital by selling a stake in your business to investors — angel investors, venture capital, private equity funds, or strategic partners. Unlike debt, there are no fixed repayments. Investors share in the upside as your business grows, making it ideal for high-growth ventures.
Key insight: Equity capital fuels exponential growth — but comes with shared ownership
