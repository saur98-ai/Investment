Step 03 — Making It Legal
Written · Witnessed · Valid
What Makes a Will Valid in India?
Under the Indian Succession Act, a valid Will must be in writing, signed by you (the testator), and witnessed by at least 2 people who are not beneficiaries. Registration is optional but strongly recommended as it prevents disputes about authenticity.
Pro tip: Register your Will at the Sub-Registrar's office for maximum legal protection
