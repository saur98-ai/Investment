Step 01 — The Basics
Fixed Income · Capital Protection
What Exactly is a Bond?
A bond is a loan you give to the government or a company. In return, they pay you regular interest (coupon) and return your principal at maturity. It's investing with a guaranteed promise.
Simple formula: You lend → They pay interest → You get principal back at maturity
